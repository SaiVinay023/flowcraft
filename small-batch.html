Our Commitment to Sustainable Clothing Packaging

FlowCraft is a sustainably-focused business. We are on course to ensure that 100% of our packaging will be reusable, recyclable, or biodegradable.

Types of Eco-Friendly Packaging We Use

  • Hemp - Durable, biodegradable, and sustainable.
  • PLA (Polylactic Acid) - Bioplastic from renewable sources like corn starch.
  • Cellulose - Plant-based, renewable, and compostable.
  • Recycled Cardboard - Gives materials a second life and reduces waste.
  • Corrugated Bubble Wrap - Compostable protective shipping material.
  • Bio-Plastics - Plant-based plastics that biodegrade fully.

Embrace Sustainability with FlowCraft

We enable our partners to stay true to eco-conscious values with reusable and recyclable packaging solutions.

We Operate Responsibly at Every Stage

From design to packaging, FlowCraft uses eco-friendly materials, low-impact printing, and recycling practices at every step.
